Projects

BikeLock

BikeLock is an iPhone app to help you find bike racks near you in Chicago. The current data for Chicago is powered by The City of Chicago.

It is no longer in the app store. I plan on releasing the source code soon. In the meantime you can view a demonstration of the app here: Youtube Video

Shotput

Shotput started out as an idea I pitched on the StartUp Bus. The idea changed as we continued to build it - but we completed a working web application in less than 48 hours. Shotput was the winner of the Chicago bus.

We were featured on the Twilio Blog for this adventure of ours!

BloNoSquare

BloNoSquare was made for a class project. Using drinks as tags on foursquare, this site allowed you to search drinks and you can find out what bars in Bloomington-Normal, IL serve that drink. It also showed you yelp reviews and foursquare data for that bar.Why Bloomington-Normal? Because that's my college town.

I used Twilio for this project, as well. By using twilio, it also let you search using SMS.

The site is currently down.

TextMeMySchedule

TextMeMySchedule was an idea I came up with a week before one of my semesters at college started. At the beginning of every semester, I would write down my school schedule. I would stare at that piece of paper until I knew it by heart.

So I thought up a solution. The user has to put in their schedule and they will get a text everyday with their classes that day. But it only lasts for 2 weeks! (By then hopefully you know your schedule!) The Twilio Blog featured me for this web application.
