Projects

BikeLock

BikeLock is an iPhone app to help you find bike racks near you in Chicago. It was created in less than 48 hours. More locations are coming soon. The current data for Chicago is powered by The City of Chicago.

Shotput

Shotput started out as an idea I pitched on the StartUp Bus. The idea changed as we continued to build it - but we completed a working web application in less than 48 hours. Shotput was the winner of the Chicago bus.

We were featured on the Twilio Blog for this adventure of ours!

BloNoSquare

BloNoSquare was made for a class project. Using drinks as tags on foursquare, this site allowed you to search drinks and you can find out what bars in Bloomington-Normal, IL serve that drink. It also showed you yelp reviews and foursquare data for that bar.Why Bloomington-Normal? Because that's my college town.

I used Twilio for this project, as well. By using twilio, it also let you search using SMS.
